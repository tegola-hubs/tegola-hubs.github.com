Community Broadband Scotland

Over the Seas From Skye

On 12 and 13 October, 2012 the Scottish Government will launch its Community Broadband Scotland initiative at Sabhal Mòr Ostaig on Skye. The event is taking place in conjunction with a celebration of the support given by Sabhal Mòr Ostaig, the University of Edinburgh and the University of the Highlands and Islands for the delivery of superfast broadband to some of the most remote communities in Scotland, which are, quite literally, over the seas from Skye.

This website is for information about the event. If you are interested in attending please look here for details of whom to contact. The programme will be posted here as it takes shape. Please note that the current structure is provisional and is there just to give a guide to the general timing.

Friday 12 October 2012

1:00pm Guests arrive (the Mallaig-Armadale Ferry arrives at 12:45)
1:30pm Welcome from the Universities and Scottish Government in the College's Talla Dhonaidh Chaimbeul (120-seater lecture hall)
2:00pm Talks to discuss goals of the meeting, the needs of community broadband initiatives and descriptions of some successful projects.
4:00pm Informal discussions and questions. Weather permitting, a brief tour of the Tegola/Hebnet equipment.
5:00pm A buffet dinner with drinks as an informal opportunity for those present to meet and discuss.
7.30 - 11.00pm Informal music and chat in the College bar at an Talla Mòr

Saturday 13 October 2012

9:00am Organised by Community Broadband Scotland to initiate further community developments and promote interaction
12:00am Departure
